# WPX Energy

WPX Energy was an independent oil and gas exploration and production company focused on the Permian and Williston basins. In January 2021, WPX Energy completed a merger with Devon Energy Corporation, forming one of the largest independent oil and gas producers in the United States.

WPX Energy does not operate as a standalone company and does not maintain a public developer API. This repository documents the company's digital presence, domain vocabulary, and linked data context for API catalog purposes.
